Typography

Headings consist of two fonts: Tolyer X Vintage No. 1 and Sketch Caslon. Tolyer X Vintage No. 1 is used in most cases, Sketch Caslon is used as an accent on H1 and H4 level headings. The body copy uses a browser-safe font - Trebuchet MS - to lessen the load on site performance speed.

Color

The primary colors used throughout the site are #fffefe, #5a534e, and #56bbb9. The secondary colors are pulled directly from the truRoots product packaging. The concept around color is to use a [blank] slate background with bold splashes color from the product packaging.

Tone & Voice

The Art Institutes brand tone should be energetic, confident, direct, & passionate. The brand personality should be curious, intelligent, creative & bold.

Imagery

  • Backgrounds can consist of slates, gray woods, dark cement, dark marbles, and used & aged wood - not overly rustic.
  • Props: old spoons, cast iron skillet, dark speckled pans, dark marble, aged silverware.
  • Use natural light or 'simulated' natural light to acheive this tone.
  • Product should be front and center - the focus of the image.
